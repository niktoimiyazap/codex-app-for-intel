# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Provide actionable feedback.
- Focus on technical outcomes, not personal attacks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Trolling, deliberate disruption, or bad-faith communication.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block contributors who violate this policy.
